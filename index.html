About

Since as early as I can remember, I have always been fascinated by computers. While in elementary school, I predicted I would study Computer Science in college. By middle school, I started to build computers by myself. In high school, I took several high level Computer Science classes. Now, I am enrolled in one of the best Computer Science programs in the state.

While my passion for Computer Science drives me eagerly toward a career in the field, I also have a few other interests. I am pursuing a minor in Creative Writing. I have always enjoyed writing and find it to be both a useful and a therapeutic form of expression. Additionally, I find Political Science very engaging, and nearly minored in the subject as a result.
